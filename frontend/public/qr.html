📱 How to Use These QR Codes

🍔 For Customers (Table QR Codes):

  1. Print the "Customer Menu" QR code (green badge)
  2. Place it on restaurant tables in small stands or laminated cards
  3. Customers scan with any QR scanner or camera app
  4. They go directly to menu → order food → get order status
  5. ✅ No login needed, instant access!

👨‍💼 For Kitchen Staff (Admin):

  1. Staff can scan the "Admin Panel" QR code (red badge)
  2. Or install Expo Go app and scan the "Expo Go" QR code
  3. Login with: admin / admin123
  4. Receive orders in real-time + manage menu

🖨️ Printing Tips:

  1. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) to print this page
  2. Or take screenshots of individual QR codes
  3. Print size: At least 2x2 inches for easy scanning
  4. Test scanning before printing multiple copies
  5. Laminate for durability on tables
💡 Pro Tip: The "Customer Menu" QR code is the most important one - print this for all your tables so customers can order instantly!
